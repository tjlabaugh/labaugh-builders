About Us

LaBaugh Builders LLC, is a full service construction company specializing in new homes and residential renovation. With over 27 years of experience we pride ourselves on quality workmanship and our ability to complete projects promptly and efficiently. Our goal and top priority is to combine your personal aspirations and desires with our vast knowledge and experience to offer you the house of your dreams!
